Event Details

“Join us for an exciting Technical Fest that brings together innovation, creativity, and talent. The fest features a 24-hour Hackathon for problem solvers, an engaging Poster Presentation to showcase innovative ideas, a Photography Contest to capture creativity through lenses, a Debate Competition to test communication and critical thinking skills, a thrilling Quiz Competition to challenge knowledge and speed, hands-on Workshops led by experts to enhance practical skills, and a grand Cultural Night filled with music, dance, and entertainment. Don’t miss this opportunity to learn, compete, connect, and celebrate technology and culture!”
